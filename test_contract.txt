EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

Clause 1 - Working Hours
The employee shall work 48 hours per week, from Sunday to Thursday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM with a one-hour break.

Clause 2 - Overtime
The employee shall not be entitled to any overtime compensation for hours worked beyond the standard work hours. All additional hours are considered voluntary.

Clause 3 - Annual Leave
The employee shall be entitled to 15 calendar days of annual leave after completing 6 months of continuous service.

Clause 4 - Termination
Either party may terminate this agreement at any time without cause by giving 7 days written notice.

Clause 5 - Non-Compete
The employee shall not work for any competitor within the UAE for a period of 5 years after termination of employment.

Clause 6 - Probation Period
The probation period shall be 9 months, during which either party may terminate with 3 days notice.

Clause 7 - End of Service Benefits
No end of service benefit shall be payable upon termination of employment for any reason.

Clause 8 - Dispute Resolution
Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be resolved by arbitration in accordance with DIFC-LCIA rules.

Clause 9 - Data Protection
The employer reserves the right to monitor all employee communications and electronic activity without prior notice.

Clause 10 - Governing Law
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates.
